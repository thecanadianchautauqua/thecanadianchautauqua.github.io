# Photo & Image Archives — Niagara-on-the-Lake & Chautauqua

A curated directory of online collections holding photographs, postcards, maps, and artwork of Niagara-on-the-Lake, the Chautauqua neighbourhood, its people, buildings, and events. Each entry gives the **host institution, a direct link, and a description of what you'll find** so the resource is useful before any image loads.

> **How to use this file.** Start with the **NOTL Museum online collection** and the **Google Arts & Culture Chautauqua exhibit** (Section 1) for Chautauqua-specific imagery; use the **regional digital libraries** (Section 2) for the broader town; use the **national/provincial archives** (Section 3) for maps, War of 1812, and deep history. Section 4 is a **named inventory of known Chautauqua images** with descriptions.

---

## 1. Primary Chautauqua & NOTL collections

### Niagara-on-the-Lake Museum — Online Collection (CatalogAccess)
**https://notlmuseum.catalogaccess.com/** *(moved from the old `niagarahistorical.pastperfectonline.com` address in 2024)*
The single most important repository for NOTL and Chautauqua images. A searchable database of **6,000+ catalogued objects, photographs, and archival records** from the Niagara Historical Society Museum (Canada's oldest local-history museum, est. 1895). A keyword search for **"Chautauqua" returns 46 records** (postcards, photographs, survey plans, pamphlets, and artefacts) — itemized with direct links in **§7** below. Records can be filtered to Objects / Photos / Archives and "only records with images." Includes the **John Burtniak Postcard Collection** (~1,000 cards, the largest single collection of NOTL postcards). High-res digital orders carry a service fee. **[NOTL Museum]**

Direct search link for Chautauqua: **https://notlmuseum.catalogaccess.com/search?query=Chautauqua&search=Chautauqua&includedFields=Objects%2CPhotos%2CArchives&page=1&size=50**

### Google Arts & Culture — "Chautauqua" exhibit (Niagara Historical Society Museum)
**https://artsandculture.google.com/story/chautauqua-niagara-historical-society-museum/yAVBx5cT--PjIg**
A curated visual story tracing Chautauqua from the Crooks estate to the modern neighbourhood, with **digitized historical photographs, portraits, maps, pamphlet pages, and postcards** — many of the only known images of the resort. See the **named inventory in Section 4** below. **[GAC]**

### Google Arts & Culture — Museum partner page
**https://artsandculture.google.com/partner/niagara-historical-society-museum**
The museum's full set of virtual exhibitions (Chautauqua and other NOTL topics), each with high-resolution zoomable images. **[GAC]**

### NOTL Museum — Research & Collections home
**https://www.notlmuseum.ca/research/**
Gateway to the research room, finding aids, and downloadable research papers (e.g. *Niagara Reminiscences*, *Notes on Pre-1840 Structures in NOTL Old Town*). Access to thousands of photographs by appointment. **[NOTL Museum]**

### NOTL Public Library — NOTL Heritage Portal
**https://www.notl.com/recreation-events/facilities/notl-public-library**
A community portal bringing together NOTL municipal, church, and genealogical records, artefacts, newspaper clippings, and **pictures and photographs** of the community in one place. **[NOTL Public Library]**

## 2. Regional digital libraries

### Historic Niagara Digital Collections — Niagara Falls (Ontario) Public Library
**https://nfpl.historicniagara.ca/**  ·  index: **https://nflibrary.ca/collections/historic-niagara-digital-collection**
A large, searchable index of the library's Local History Collection: **historical images, postcards, negatives, daguerreotypes**, a newspaper index, and an art collection (prints, watercolours) covering the whole Niagara frontier — including NOTL, fires, and lake steamers. Searchable by keyword (e.g. "fire," "Chautauqua," "Niagara-on-the-Lake"). **[NFPL]**

### Brock University Library — Archives & Special Collections / Digital Exhibits
**https://brocku.ca/library/archives/digital-exhibits/**  ·  exhibits portal: **https://exhibits.library.brocku.ca/**
Millions of records on Niagara history. Highlights:
- **Historic Houses in Niagara** — photographs of historic homes and their residents: **https://exhibits.library.brocku.ca/s/historic-houses-in-niagara/item**
- **Niagara Camp postcards** — 39 postcards of the NOTL military training camp (curated by Chantal Cameron).
- **George E. Curtis: Photographs of Niagara c. 1860–1870** — early photographs of the Niagara region: **https://exhibits.library.brocku.ca/s/george-e-curtis-photographs-of-niagara-ca1860-1870/page/introduction**
- Thematic **Niagara postcard** exhibits (tourism, street scenes, special events). **[Brock]**

### Niagara-on-the-Lake Realty / ExploringNiagara — illustrated local histories
**https://www.exploringniagara.com/niagara_on_the_lake/chautauqua.html** (Chautauqua/Mississauga Beach, with period imagery and maps) and **https://www.notlrealty.com/niagara-historic-sights/** (illustrated guide to historic sights). Useful secondary galleries with captions. **[EN]**

## 5a. Verified, downloaded images in this collection

A focused hunt (documented in **[FINDINGS-amphitheatre.md](FINDINGS-amphitheatre.md)**) retrieved and verified several period images from the NOTL Museum's curated Google Arts & Culture exhibit. These are saved locally in `photos/` and include the prize find:

- **`amphitheatre_NOTLMuseum_GAC_interior.jpg`** — *The one known photograph of the Chautauqua amphitheatre* (interior): an open timber pavilion, tiered benches, decorated stage with piano, Union Jack and **CLSC** banner, captioned *"AMPHITHEATRE (SEATING CAPACITY 4,000)."* — [GAC asset](https://artsandculture.google.com/asset/the-chautauqua-amphitheatre/OgFTerTCEvwAww). **This disproves the common belief that no amphitheatre photo survives.**
- **`chautauqua_map-of-grounds_amphitheatre-hub_NOTLMuseum.jpg`** — *"Map of Grounds Belonging to The Niagara (late Fort George) Assembly… The Canadian Chautauqua"* (Sproatt, VanNostrand, Toronto): the full spoke-and-wheel plan with the **amphitheatre drawn at the hub** and every avenue named — [GAC asset](https://artsandculture.google.com/asset/map-of-the-canadian-chautauqua/IQHJdG1mX6KXHg).
- **`chautauqua_hotel_postcard_NOTLMuseum.jpg`** — colour postcard of the three-storey **Hotel Chautauqua** with lakeside domed pavilion — [GAC asset](https://artsandculture.google.com/asset/chautauqua-hotel/jAGPb6UDyxG7ng).
- **`chautauqua_lakefront-boathouse_NOTLMuseum.jpg`** — the lakefront **boat/bath house** with rowboats and visitors (Ryerson Park waterfront) — [GAC asset](https://artsandculture.google.com/asset/ryerson-park/GgHR6y4dA94odQ).
- **`chautauqua_ryerson-park-group_c1890_NOTLMuseum.jpg`** — group portrait under the trees beneath a **"RYERSON PARK"** sign, c. 1890 — [GAC asset](https://artsandculture.google.com/asset/group-of-unknown-individuals-at-ryerson-park-c-1890/EwE0ogX3AzCgQA).
- **`chautauqua_assembly-grounds_engraving_1888pamphlet_NOTLMuseum.jpg`** — engraving of the grounds (cottage, pavilion, tents) from the *Season of 1888* programme — [GAC asset](https://artsandculture.google.com/asset/view-of-the-assembly-grounds/gAF9Joc4cblFAw).

## 5b. Wider aggregators & the "lost amphitheatre" photo hunt

Treating NOTL as a hub, these large aggregators were searched for **Chautauqua-neighbourhood** imagery the local museum may not hold — especially an **exterior photograph of the 4,000-seat amphitheatre**. The museum holds **one interior photograph** (retrieved above); **no exterior photograph is known** to survive in any collection searched.

- **New York Public Library — Digital Collections** — https://digitalcollections.nypl.org/
- **Library of Congress — Prints & Photographs** — https://www.loc.gov/photos/
- **Digital Public Library of America (DPLA)** — https://dp.la/
- **New York Heritage Digital Collections** — https://nyheritage.org/
- **Wikimedia Commons** — https://commons.wikimedia.org/
- **Internet Archive** (Chautauqua-movement books with assembly plates) — https://archive.org/ (e.g. **Vincent, *The Chautauqua Movement*** https://archive.org/details/chautauquamoveme00vinc)

> **Hunt result (this session):** these collections are rich in **Chautauqua *Institution* (New York)** amphitheatre images (e.g. NYPL ["The Amphitheatre, Chautauqua Institution"](https://digitalcollections.nypl.org/items/510d47d9-9dbe-a3d9-e040-e00a18064a99); LoC ["Interior of Assembly Hall, Chautauqua, N.Y."](https://www.loc.gov/item/2016794166/)) — **useful as visual analogues** for what the Niagara amphitheatre looked like, but **none depicts the Niagara-on-the-Lake amphitheatre**. As of this research the **Niagara amphitheatre's exterior remains undocumented photographically**; the most promising untried leads are period **Toronto/Buffalo postcard publishers** and the **Chautauqua Institution Archives** correspondence files (a daughter-assembly may appear in parent records). See [external-archives-source-map.md](../docs/external-archives-source-map.md).
