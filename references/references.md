## Notes on reliability & corroboration
- **Institutional** sources (NOTL Museum, Parks Canada, Brock, LAC, GAC exhibit) are preferred for facts; **commercial** travel/realty pages are used for colour and corroboration and flagged where they are the sole source.
- Known **conflicts** (Crooks grantee, acreage, 1887 vs 1888 first season, Shaw Festival founding year) are flagged inline in the relevant docs and in each file's *Open questions* section. The **NOTL Museum research room** and the **Map of the Canadian Chautauqua** are the recommended primary sources to resolve them.
- All URLs verified reachable during compilation on **2026-06-30**.
